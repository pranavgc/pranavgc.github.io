May 2021 – Jun 2024 Deep Learning Physics

Particle Identification in CMS/HGCAL via Graph Neural Networks

MS thesis research at IISER Kolkata & Saha Institute of Nuclear Physics. Developed supervised ML models for particle identification based on shower shapes in the High Granularity Calorimeter of the Compact Muon Solenoid (CMS) experiment.
